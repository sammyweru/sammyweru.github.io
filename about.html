About Me

I’m Samuel Chege Waweru, a self-taught cybersecurity enthusiast based in Kenya. My journey started with curiosity — wanting to understand how systems are broken, and how to secure them. That curiosity has grown into a disciplined mission to help secure digital systems across Africa and beyond.

I’m currently building hands-on skills through:

  • CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) training
  • Simulating enterprise networks using Cisco Packet Tracer
  • Using tools like SpiderFoot, Shodan, Recon-ng, h8mail
  • Practicing vulnerability scanning and network security design
  • Learning Python to automate OSINT and recon tasks

I believe in ethical responsibility, deep learning, and building for long-term impact — especially in underserved digital communities. “In a world full of threats, be the firewall.” This isn’t just a career — it’s my calling.
